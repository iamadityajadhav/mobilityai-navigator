# 🚄 MobilityAI Navigator

**AI Readiness Assessment, Use Case Recommender & Adoption Playbook for Rail & Mobility**

A consulting-grade tool that automates three core workflows: assessing AI readiness, recommending prioritized use cases, and designing adoption strategies — specifically calibrated for rail and mobility organizations.

## Features

### Module 1: AI Readiness Assessment
- 8-dimension assessment adapted for rail & mobility (including Safety & Regulatory, Cross-Org Collaboration)
- Interactive radar chart with rail industry benchmarks
- Maturity level classification (5 levels: Exploring → Transforming)
- Gap analysis with strengths and improvement areas

### Module 2: Use Case Recommender
- 40+ real AI implementations from global rail operators (DB, SNCF, SBB, MTR, JR East, Trenitalia, Knorr-Bremse, Optibus)
- Filterable by S3 pillar (Punctuality, Reliability, Economic Efficiency), category, and complexity
- Impact vs. Feasibility matrix for prioritization
- Personalized recommendations based on readiness score

### Module 3: Community Adoption Playbook
- Tailored AI adoption strategies per maturity level
- Champion network design with target ratios
- 90-day action plan with concrete activities
- KPIs for measuring adoption success

### PDF Report Generation
- Consulting-quality PDF export
- Executive summary, dimension scores, recommended use cases, action plan

### Bilingual Support
- Full German and English language toggle

## Methodology

- **Assessment Framework:** Synthesized from Gartner (AI Maturity Model), Cisco (AI Readiness Index, 8,000-company benchmark), and McKinsey (Rewired), adapted with two rail-specific dimensions
- **Use Case Database:** Curated from 7+ international rail operators, aligned to DB's S3 strategy pillars
- **Community Playbook:** Informed by enterprise AI adoption programs at Citi, PwC, Accenture, and DB's Power Platform community
